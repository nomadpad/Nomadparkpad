Common Host Questions

Do I have to socialize with travelers?

No. You choose your Host Style. A safe, quiet driveway with clear instructions is a complete and valuable stay.

Can I control who books?

The planned marketplace lets hosts review requests and traveler information before accepting.

Can I choose my own price and availability?

Yes. Hosts set their price, available dates, arrival times, vehicle limits, rules, and amenities.

Is Nomad Park Pad already accepting bookings?

Not yet. The platform is in early development and is currently recruiting founding hosts and travelers.
