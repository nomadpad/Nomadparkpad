FAQ

Early questions, straight answers.

Is Nomads Park Pad live yet?

We are building the early launch list now. Hosts and travelers can join first and help shape the platform.

What can hosts offer?

Driveway space, parking instructions, power, water, pet-friendly access, or other extras they choose to provide.

What vehicles will be allowed?

The first version is built around vans, cars, small RVs, truck campers, and travel trailers, with host-set size limits.

Where will it launch first?

We are starting with Alberta and nearby travel routes, then expanding as host supply grows.
